Rambling about Diversity and Inclusion in Tech

One of the things I'd like to do here is to create a safe place for developers (new, old, and those that want to be) to chat, share ideas, trials, success stories, anything really. We were all code newbies at one point.

I firmly believe that we should all have the same opportunities regardless of the gender we identify with, the color of our skin, our religion, or, for those fortunate enough, if we are a healthy person in recovery. It does not and should not matter and should never be a deciding factor in our pursuit of success in tech (or life)! We love technology and programming the same, so why should anything separate us?
